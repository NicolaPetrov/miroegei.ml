The Begining

Back Story
For more than four million years the Monkes have lived in harmony with other beings on our planet - Earth. Until about one million years ago the humans "evolved" from our grandfathers - the Monkes.

With the so called "human evolution", more like "deevolution", the human species, unlike the Monkes became stupider and stupider. They started colonizing other continents, killing each other, using more than they needed and destroyed their own mother - the Mother Earth.

With their "evolution" the human species achieved only one thing - they became Evil, Greedier and Stupider. And now it was only a matter of time for mankind to destroy itself.

Monke Revolution

Monke Spirit
As time passed, the Monkes became more and more fed up with the greedy and evil human race. They slowly became ashamed of what their original children have become. With the time the shame became so unbearable, so the Monkes decided its time for a change.

A change for the better, a change which should end all suffering and misery around the world. And so they called. It was a stronk message, message uniting all the monkes around the world, a message asking for help for a better tomorrow.

The Call
The call for help spreaded like a pandemic around the world. Soon every monke have heard the message. The stronk message combined with the uncrushed monke spirit, easily lit the fighting spirit inside all the monkes around the world.
